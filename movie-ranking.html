My top 5 movies of all time

Interstellar

Its a 2014 epic sci-fi film directed by my all time fav Chris Nolan and starring Mathew McConaghey

The Wolf of Wall Street

Its a 2013 biographical film based on the life of Jordqn Belfort a.k.a Wolfie.
Directed by Martin Scorcease and starring Leonardo DiCaprio

La La Land

Its a 2016 musical rom-com directed by Damien Chazelle.
Film stars Ryan Gosling and Emma Stone as two young individuals with aspiring ambitions who fall in love while chasing their dreams
(btw rom-com is my fav genre)

Kabhi Khushi Kabhi Gham

Also known K3G,is a 2001 family drama directed by Karan Johar
Starring SRK,Kajol and Amitabhi Bachcan in lead roles.The film revolves around an Indian family facing issues with their adopted son marrige
According to me its the family film made till date

Ae Dil Hai Mushkil

Its a 2016 rom-com directed by Karan Johar
Starring Ranbir Kapoor,Anushka Sharma and Aishwarya Rai.Story is about one sided love and friendship
The movie is like revisiting Kuch Kuch Hota Hai but with a more turbulent emotional rollercoaster!
